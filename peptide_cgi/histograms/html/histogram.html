Chemical Shift Distribution Histograms

Note: you can drill down on a histogram bar to view a dialog that, among other information, has links to the entries contributing to that histogram bucket. To drill down, switch to drill-down mode (select this icon) in the toolbar on the left side of the JavaScreen, and then left-click on the desired bar. For these links to work, you may have to turn off popup blocking in your web browser.
