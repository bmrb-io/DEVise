Relaxation Paraemters

These are backbone 15N relaxation parameters for individual residues. The top graph is 1/T1 at 750 MHz, the second is 1/T2 at 750 MHz, the third is 1/T2 at 600 MHz, and the bottom shows 1H-15N NOE values at 750 MHz. Gaps correspond to prolines or to residues whose values could not be measured because of spectral overlap.
