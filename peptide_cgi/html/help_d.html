Chemical Shift Delta

The plotted chemical shift differences are to be used in conjunction with the chemical shift index plots provided for this entry. They have been calculated using the observed chemical shifts reported in the entry and the standard chemical shifts reported by Wishart and coworkers (see http://www.bmrb.wisc.edu/ref_info/csishift.txt).

Note that for cysteine, the standard chemical shift values for CB (oxidized, 41.8 ppm) are used as opposed to the reduced values.

References

  1. Wishart, D.S., Sykes, B.D., and Richards, F.M., 'Relationship between Nuclear Magnetic Resonance Chemical Shift and Protein Secondary Structure,' J. Mol. Biol. 222, 311-333 (1991)
  2. Wishart, D.S. and Sykes, B.D., 'The 13C Chemical-Shift Index: A simple method for the identification of protein secondary structure using 13C chemical shift data,' J. Biomol. NMR 4, 171-180 (1994).
