The format for the filenames are XY.dat,
where X is the accession number, and Y is one of the following letters:
	c	csi
	d	deltashift
	f	hydrogen exchange protection factor
	g	coupling constants
	n	NOE
	o	t1
	p	percentage of atoms assigned
	r	hydrogen exchange rate
	s	s2
	t	t2
	u	t2_600

The summary page is Xy.html
