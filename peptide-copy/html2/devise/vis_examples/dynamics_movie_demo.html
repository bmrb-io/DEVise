Demo molecular dynamics visualizations


The visualizations linked below show predicted vs. experimental S2 order parameters, linked to molecular dynamics movies generated from simulations (not uploaded data of actual trajectories). When you view one of the visualizations, you can select either a "generic" movie of the whole molecule, or a shorter movie that highlights the residue you select in the visualization.
