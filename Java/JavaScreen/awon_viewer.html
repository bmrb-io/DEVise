Interactive Weather Data Browser

This page is used for graphically browsing surface weather data collected by the Department of Soil Science, Unversity of Wisconsin-Madison. The graphs are generated using DEVise. Here you can view several years of weather observations collected at weather stations around Wisconsin by clicking your mouse button or using just single keys on your keyboard.

The development of this interactive data browser is a team effort between environmental researchers in the Soil Science Department and the DEVise Development Group in the Computer Science Department at the University of Wisconsin-Madions.

To use this interactive web site, you must have Netscape 4.08 or greater installed on your computer.
